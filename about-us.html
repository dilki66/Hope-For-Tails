Who We Are

Hope for Tails is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming cats and dogs in need. Our mission is to create a world where every pet has a loving home and every home knows the joy of a furry friend.

We work closely with volunteers, veterinarians, and foster families to ensure the health and happiness of every pet we rescue.
